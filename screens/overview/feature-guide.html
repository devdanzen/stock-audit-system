Quick Feature Guide

What each screen does, in plain English
Screen / Feature What It Does
DashboardHomepage with daily summary and quick alerts
Master Data
ItemsThe catalog of everything the restaurant sells
CategoriesGroups items by section (Bar, Food, Kitchen, Sushi)
OutletsAll restaurant locations in the chain
VendorsSuppliers we buy from
UsersStaff accounts with login access
Transactions
ReceivingRecord incoming deliveries — stock goes UP
SalesRecord customer purchases — stock goes DOWN
MovementRecord stock changes that aren't sales: waste, transfers between outlets, internal use
RecipesHow each dish breaks down into ingredients — selling one dish deducts all its ingredients from stock
Stock Operations
Stock On HandLive view of how much of everything we have now
AuditWalk the storage, count items, system flags any differences as inventory variance
End BalanceLock in the month's closing inventory value for financial reports
Reports & Charts
ReportsStock cards, sales summaries, receiving logs — printable for management
ChartsTop sellers, daily sales trend, sales by category, top waste items — at a glance
Common Terms
SOH (Stock On Hand)
Current quantity of an item in storage right now
M-M Relationship
When two things relate to many of each other (e.g. one invoice has many items, one item appears on many invoices)
Audit
Physical inventory count to verify accuracy
Movement
Any stock change that's not a sale or receipt (waste, transfer, consumption)
